CLEAR™ Execution Framework

Turning good intentions into consistent results in volunteer-driven organizations

Created by Leo Smith, DTM

The Problem CLEAR™ Solves

Volunteer organizations are filled with talented, committed people who care deeply about their mission. Yet many struggle with inconsistent execution, role confusion, and burnout.

Motivation is not the issue. Design is.

CLEAR™ is designed for environments where authority is limited — but results still matter.

The CLEAR™ Framework

  • Clarity — Define outcomes, roles, and ownership before activity begins.
  • Leverage — Replace heroics with simple, repeatable systems.
  • Execution — Make the right behavior the default behavior.
  • Accountability — Create visibility and feedback without micromanagement.
  • Results — Measure what actually changed, not just what was attempted.
